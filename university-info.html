      COURSE

      Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

      Overview ::

      BSc (IT) is 3 year duration undergraduate programme which is sub-divided into 6 semesters. This course primarily focuses on the processing and management of information in a systematic way.


      DRJ TechSoft is a well reputed name for BSc(IT) course. We offer BSc IT programme for those, who want to pursue a career in information technology (IT) sector. The course duration is 3 year or 6 semesters and students will learn data structure & algorithms using C, technical communication skills, web programming, computer organisation and architecture etc. The programme is blend of both the theoretical and practical knowledge. Graduates with BSc IT are able to perform technology tasks related to the processing, storing & communication between computer, mobiles, and other electronic devices.


      Course Duration ::

      3 Years


      Eligibility ::

      12th any stream


      Course Type ::

      Regular/ODL (Semester Based)


      Placement & Career ::

      BSc IT graduates can get an initial annual salary package between 2.5 lakhs to 4 lakhs per annum. Candidates with a BSc IT degree can get profiles such as programmer, system engineer, software engineer, network administrator.


      Reward ::

      BSC(IT) Degree
